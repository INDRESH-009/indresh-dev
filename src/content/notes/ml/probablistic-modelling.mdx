## Core idea
Machine learning is more coherent when framed as **inference under uncertainty**.

Inline:
$ p(\theta \,|\, D) $

Display:

$$
p(\theta \,|\, D) = \frac{p(D \,|\, \theta)p(\theta)}{p(D)}
$$
